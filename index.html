WHO WE ARE

Pioneering Global Logistics

Doverseas is a global logistics and freight forwarding company headquartered in New Delhi, India. We specialize in custom clearance, international freight forwarding, and import-export operations. With a vast network across Canada, China, Thailand, Germany, USA, Russia, Hong Kong, Korea, Indonesia, UK, and all of Europe, we ensure seamless international trade for businesses of all sizes.

Mr. Deepanshu Goel

Founder & CEO

With over a decade of experience in global logistics and trade regulations, Deepanshu Goel founded Doverseas with the vision of simplifying cross-border commerce. Under his leadership, the company has scaled into a global brand, offering premium and reliable solutions for international businesses.
